Visual Aid Prototype Site

Hello, welcome to our proto-site for making lectures more accessible to those with vision impairments.

With this site you can upload your lecture to the site and our VLM will join you in watching and assist in helping identify and understand what images, graphs, charts and other visual aids are present in the lecture and what they mean in the context of the lecture.

At the moment this site is not actaully capable of generating descriptions, but serves as an idea of what this "product" can look like with more development. We have created a more streamlined version of what the site can possibly look like and have created multiple mock scenarios to demonstrate our vision for what this site can possibly be.

Different example how this site will be able to be used are linked below.

How to Use this Site

Here is a helpful guide on how to use this site.

Uploading your lecture

To upload your lecture, there is an input box, that asks for a URL link. Simply post the link to you live talk in the "Add link" and press enter or click Submit, to have the aid be able to access the lecture. Click here to input your link now. (possibly change if we want the user to upload a video file instead of a zoom link or even have permisson to allow site to access your screen and microphone)

Note: Under construction, does not actually do anything

Generating and Speaking Descripton

After connecting to lecture (live or recorded), the aid will generate descriptions as it detects them. The most recent description will be labeled as "Current Description" and will be updated as more descriptions are generated. Once a new description is generated the old one will be added to a transcript of descriptions that can be refered back to. To have the current description read press [KEY] to have it be read to you.
