Visual Aid Prototype Site

Hello, welcome to our proto-site for making lectures more accessible to those with vision impairments.

With this site you can upload your lecture to the site and our VLM will join you in watching and assist in helping identify and understand what images, graphs, charts and other visual aids are present in the lecture and what they mean in the context of the lecture.

At the moment this site is not actaully capable of generating descriptions, but serves as an idea of what this "product" can look like with more development. We have created a more streamlined version of what the site can possibly look like and have created multiple mock scenarios to demonstrate our vision for what this site can possibly be.

How to Use this Site

Here is a helpful guide on how to use this site.

Uploading your lecture

To upload your lecture, there is an input box, that asks for a URL link. Simply post the link to you live talk in the URL input box labeled "Lecture link", and press enter or click the button "Submit lecture and contuine", to have the aid be able to access the lecture. After submitting lecutre you will be redirected to the description page, where the aid will be providing descriptions on visual information as it is detected.

Generating and Speaking Descripton

After connecting to lecture (live or recorded), the aid will generate descriptions as it detects them playing a "Ding" audio cue for each new generated description. The most recent description can be read by clicking the "Play Audio Description" button. The description block will be updated as more descriptions are generated. The user will be able to go back and have previous description be read out loud by clicking the "Previous Description" and "Next Description" buttons. For demonstation purposes we have also added a "New Description" button to force genrate a new description.

How to use simulated examples

Included in this prototype to help demonstrate our hopes for this webiste are mock scenarios that showcase: how the site will notify a user of descriptions and what the content of the descriptions will be.

Each scenario will have an audio recorindg of a fake short presenation that describes figures and images not shown or described. When the sepaker indicates that an visual is being described, an audio cue will play and a description will be played. Due to the generation not being implamented we have hard coded the cues and descriptions. To try out the simulated examples yourself!

Note: Many features are not operational, as this site is just a prototype
